What ways do we have to save the environment?

Plastic Usage

Would you like a plastic [anything]? Say No! One small step goes a long way for nature.

Volunteering

Got spare time over the weekend? You can volunteer to make your beach a better place to enjoy and relax.

Aquariums

What better way to familiarize yourself with the ocean than going to the aquarium? Immerse yourself in the beautiful and fascinating sight of aquatic animals!
